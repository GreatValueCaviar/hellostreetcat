Meet Mr. Piss from Hello Street Cat

Meet Mr. Piss, the most beloved street cat in the neighborhood! With his fluffy calico fur and captivating green eyes, he's hard to miss.

Despite his name, Mr. Piss is a true sweetheart. He earned his unique moniker from a mischievous incident involving a leaking garden hose, but don't let it fool you - he's as charming as can be!

Mr. Piss is a curious explorer, always sniffing out new adventures and forging connections with everyone he meets. He's particularly fond of sunbathing on warm afternoons and chasing after fluttering butterflies.

When he's not busy patrolling the streets or cozying up in a sunny spot, Mr. Piss can often be found cuddled up with his human friends, purring contentedly as they scratch behind his ears.

Join Mr. Piss on his delightful escapades through Hello Street Cat, where every day is filled with joy, friendship, and the occasional misadventure!
