Mrs. Kirby

In the vibrant world of Hello Street, there lived a remarkable feline known as Mrs. Kirby. She was a short-haired tabby calico, mostly white with a distinctive grey spot on the top of her head and orange ear tips. Another unique feature was a black spot on her nose. But the most notable mark of all was the Kirby-shaped grey and orange spot on her back, which inspired her name, Mrs. Kirby.

Initially known as Ms. Kirby, her considerable size was soon understood to be due to her pregnancy. Around mid March of 2024, she gave birth, and her title was changed to Mrs. Kirby, reflecting her new status as a mother.

Mrs. Kirby frequented a feeder known as the Happy Canteen, where she reigned supreme as the queen. Her territorial nature was well-known, and she was quick to assert her dominance whenever another cat approached the feeder. This assertiveness earned her a formidable reputation, as she picked fights with many of the other cats in the area, including Mr. Fresh, Mr. Fluff, Mr. Piss, Mr. Beef, Mr. Bald, Mr. Airplane, Ms. Chess, Mr. Croissant, Dr. Smudge, Mr. Void, Ms. Puke, Mr. Egg, Mr. Chicken, Goobert, and Mrs. Inception, among others. Her prowess in these skirmishes earned her the reputation of a professional boxer.

Despite her fierce demeanor, Mrs. Kirby had a soft spot for a cat known as the Gluttonous Beast, refusing to fight him. Another cat who seemed to have an influence over her was Mr. Doom, who managed to intimidate her on one occasion.

With her regal appearance and assertive personality, Mrs. Kirby became a beloved and respected figure in the Hello Street community. Her story is one of strength, motherhood, and the indomitable spirit of a true queen.
